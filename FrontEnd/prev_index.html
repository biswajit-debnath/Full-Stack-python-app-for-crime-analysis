ABOUT OUR SYSTEM

Crime rate prediction is a systematic approach for identifying and analysing patterns and rate in crime. To be better prepared to respond to criminal activity, it is important to understand patterns in crime.

The objective is to analysis of crime data from all the states of north-east India and their districts, scraped from publicly available website data.gov.in.

SYSTEM CAPABILITY

The system is able to predict regions which have high rate of crime occurrence, analyse the trend of each on yeary basis, visualize the crime prone areas using map or barplots and further analysis can be carried out.
